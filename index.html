About The Game

Bonk is an upcoming 2D online multiplayer brawler where you must 'bonk' your enemies (or friends) on their head.

What can you do in Bonk?

Here are the following features as of the latest version:

Up to four players play in a 2D map that contains platforms and other obstacles they must avoid bonking their heads on. There are many modes to choose from including: a classic mode, power-ups mode and many more! You can play the game online, in a custom game with friends, or offline with bots!

  • Fast-paced fun gameplay with your friends.
  • Many different mechanics with a tutorial to teach you how to use them.
  • Free to play gameplay with paid accessories for your character.

Features

Fun Brawler Based Gameplay

Battle it out with friends by trying to bonk them straight onto their heads.

Exciting Gamemodes

Many different gamemodes to keep you on your toes: Classic, Powerups, 8-Player, Teams, Teams with Powerups, and so many more!

Online and Offline Games

Lobbies to invite your friends and only your friends to! Or if you are feeling lonely, find other people's games or play offline with bots.

Free to Play

All of these features and more for free! But you can still support us with in-game purchases.
